My Priness

You are my princess
So dear and true
The one who brightens my day
And makes me smile too

You are my princess
With your eyes like the night sky
They twinkle and shine
To you I could not lie

You are my princess
With your beauty so bright and bold
You make my body warm
When I am cold

You are my princess
The heavens have opened up
And let an Angel like you
Stir my love up
Oh, You are my princess
With all of this true
Because you are my princess
And I will always treat you like one too.

by Ryan Thompson

All my thoughts are with you
as I send you my love and warmth.
In spirit and true love I wrap you up
in my caring loving arms.
You are my beautiful Queen
I so adore and deeply love.
Kira you are my beautiful Angel Queen
sent from God up above.

You are my beautiful Queen who
I want to be with for all time.
You are the beautiful Queen that
gives me butterflies.
My soul and my heart will forever be yours
because I will always love you my
beautiful Angel and Queen forevermore.

Michael P. McParland
